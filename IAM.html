The Need for Identity and Access Management (IAM) Solutions

In today’s hyper-connected world, securely managing digital identities and access is vital for enterprise resilience. As seasoned partners of ManageEngine, Saasify implements and supports their IAM suite — such as AD360 and Identity360 — to help businesses automate provisioning, enforce SSO & MFA, monitor privileged access, and streamline compliance through real-time reporting and behavioral analytics. This ensures only the right people have access, at the right time, with maximum security and zero complexity.

Our IAM Capabilities

Key IAM Capabilities That Drive Success

Built-in Universal Directory

As a certified ManageEngine partner, we help businesses implement their cloud-native universal directory. This allows organizations to store and manage identities across multiple directories and applications seamlessly. We ensure that user data is synchronized in real-time across all connected platforms.

Identity Orchestration & Lifecycle Automation

We assist in automating onboarding, provisioning, role changes, and offboarding using ManageEngine IAM workflows. This reduces human error, speeds up user access provisioning, and helps maintain a consistent security posture. Our team supports implementation and optimization to improve operational efficiency.

Secure SSO with MFA

We guide organizations to implement ManageEngine's single sign-on and multi-factor authentication features. Employees can log in once and access authorized applications securely, boosting productivity while reducing credential theft risks.

Access Insights & Compliance Reporting

Through our partnership with ManageEngine, we help organizations gain insights with audit-ready reports and dashboards. Quickly identify unusual access patterns, stay compliant with regulations like GDPR, HIPAA, and ISO, and proactively address potential threats.
